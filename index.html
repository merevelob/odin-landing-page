Dog Breeds

The Bulldog is a British breed of dog of mastiff type. It weighs around 18 to 25 kg.
The Labrador Retriever, or simply Labrador, is a British breed of retriever gun dog.
The Rottweiler is a German breed of dog, regarded as medium-to-large or large.
The Saint Bernard is a breed from the Western Alps in Italy and Switzerland.
